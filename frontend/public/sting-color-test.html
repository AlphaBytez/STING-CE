Summary

All color combinations in the STING design system meet or exceed WCAG AA standards (4.5:1 for normal text, 3:1 for large text).

  • ✅ The new warm jade green (#5d9b63) passes AA standards on all dark backgrounds
  • ✅ Primary yellow maintains excellent contrast with black text
  • ✅ All text colors meet accessibility requirements
  • ✅ Semantic colors (error, warning, info) all pass contrast tests
